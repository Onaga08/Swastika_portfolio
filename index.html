As a dedicated oral and maxillofacial surgeon, my primary goal is to provide patients with the highest standard of care, delivered with a focus on comfort and pain-free treatment. I firmly believe that successful treatment is not just about the technical skill required, but also about fostering a relationship built on trust, communication, and genuine care. I take the time to understand each patient's unique needs, ensuring they feel heard, respected, and supported throughout their entire treatment journey.

Over the years, I have had the privilege of performing a wide range of complex procedures, honing my skills in various aspects of oral and maxillofacial surgery. From the precise and skillful removal of impacted wisdom teeth to the management of intricate maxillofacial trauma, my expertise spans a broad spectrum. I have treated patients with conditions like mucocele using advanced laser techniques, providing minimally invasive solutions that ensure quicker recovery times and better outcomes.

In addition to my work with soft tissue conditions, I have extensive experience performing diagnostic and therapeutic biopsies, allowing me to address potential concerns with the utmost care and accuracy. I am also well-versed in orthognathic surgery, helping patients achieve improved functionality and aesthetics through corrective jaw procedures. My practice includes a variety of minor oral surgeries, as well as surgical interventions that are an integral part of orthodontic treatment plans.

I look forward to continuing to serve my patients with dedication, expertise, and a deep understanding of their needs. Your comfort and well-being are my top priorities, and I am here to provide you with the best care every step of the way.
